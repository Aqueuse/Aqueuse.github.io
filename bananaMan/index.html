The Baboon Update

    * A new type of ammo for each major update (including a grapple).
    * Sounds for the new ammo.
    * A new monkey.
    * A new map.
    * Music for the map.
    * A new platform behavior.
    * A new section of the corolla: shooting range.
    * Sounds for the section.

The Capuchin Update

    * A new type of ammo for each major update (including a grapple).
    * Sounds for the new ammo.
    * A new monkey.
    * A new map.
    * Music for the map.
    * A new platform behavior.
    * A new section of the corolla: restaurant.
    * Sounds for the section.

    The restaurant allows players to cook by combining bananas.
    It is run by a mini chimp who serves dishes at the table.
    These dishes give specific buffs.

The Miss Chiquita Update

    * A new type of ammunition with each major update (including a grappling hook)
    * Sounds for the new ammunition
    * A new monkey
    * A new map
    * Map music
    * A new platform behavior

    Chance of encountering Miss Chiquita in the Corolla (defeating her and making all the monkeys happy
    allows the player to become the monkey king)

    When the player becomes the monkey king, they can invite the monkey of their choice
    into the Corolla (until they run out of bananas),
    and there will be specific behaviors for each neighborhood, so the player can take a walk with their chosen monkey.
